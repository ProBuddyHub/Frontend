Frequently Asked Questions

What is StartupHub and who is it for? ▼

StartupHub is a launchpad and ecosystem for entrepreneurs to build, validate, and scale their startups. Ideal for early-stage founders, students, and innovators.

Do I need to pay to join the accelerator? ▼

No, it’s completely free. We support founders by offering resources, mentorship, and investor access with zero equity or fees.

What kind of startups are accepted? ▼

We welcome startups from all sectors — tech, sustainability, education, fintech, health, and more. The only requirement is originality and commitment.

Can I apply as a student or solo founder? ▼

Absolutely! Many of our successful alumni started as students or solo founders. We help you build your team and refine your idea.
